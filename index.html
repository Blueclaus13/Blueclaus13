Cleaning line

At Honeywell Aerospace in 2017-2018, I designed around a year a nitric cleaning line. It was a project to fix some issues in the analysis of resistance of metal pieces. The analysis technics had hazards when the employee poured the acid into a small box of plastic. In addition, sometimes the employee leaves the pieces into the acid more time than necessary. I designed as a solution a sequence process with a timer and programing monitor of acid concentration. I got recognition in Honeywell to develop a project to protect the health of the employee and to reduce expenses in the factory.
